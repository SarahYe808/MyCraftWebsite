What inspired me to start my hobbies?

Crochet

My sister recieved many handmade stuffed animals from her friend who crochets. I wanted to be the type of friend who can give handmade products.

Wood Carving

My dad left my mom and I unsupervised for 3 hours and we decided that I was going to start wood carving.

Clay Charms

I started making clay charms at a very young age when my parents bought me tools from Micheals. I wanted to make minature figureines and keychains.
